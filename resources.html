Finding the right kind of help can be confusing or discouraging, especially when you're already struggling. That's why we've created this carefully curated list of resources to guide you toward support that's actually accessible and meaningful.

Your mental health matters. Here are carefully selected resources to support your emotional well-being:

Anonymous chat with trained listeners, plus therapy options and forums.

Free meditation, mindfulness, anxiety, and sleep tracks.
🏳️‍🌈 Trevor Project
LGBTQ+ youth crisis support via text, chat, or call.

Find culturally competent therapists who match your needs.
💻 LifeStance Health
Mental health services with in-person and online therapy options nationwide.
