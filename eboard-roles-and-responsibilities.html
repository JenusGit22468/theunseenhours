Executive Board Roles & Responsibilities

Building a community of support, understanding, and growth

Our Mission

The Unseen Hours E-Board works collaboratively to create safe spaces for students navigating life's invisible battles. Each role is designed to support our community through different aspects of mental health awareness, peer support, and wellness programming. We believe in shared leadership where every voice matters and every contribution makes a difference.

President

Executive Leadership

  • Lead weekly E-Board meetings and monthly general meetings
  • Serve as primary liaison with university administration
  • Oversee strategic planning and organizational vision
  • Support and coordinate all E-Board members
  • Represent The Unseen Hours at campus events
  • Ensure alignment with mission and values

VP of Community Engagement & Outreach

Programming Leadership

  • Plan and execute community service projects
  • Develop partnerships with local organizations
  • Coordinate outreach initiatives and awareness campaigns
  • Organize workshops and guest speaker events
  • Build relationships with other campus organizations

VP of Member Relations

Member Support

  • Develop member retention and engagement strategies
  • Organize small group discussions and peer circles
  • Conduct regular member check-ins and feedback sessions
  • Create welcoming environment for new members
  • Coordinate peer mentoring programs

VP of Academic Support & First-Gen Advocacy

Academic Wellness

  • Develop resources for academic stress management
  • Create first-generation student support programs
  • Organize study groups and academic workshops
  • Address imposter syndrome through targeted programming
  • Connect students with campus academic resources

Secretary

Administrative

  • Record and distribute meeting minutes
  • Manage organizational calendar and scheduling
  • Maintain member database and attendance records
  • Handle official correspondence and communications
  • Organize digital files and documentation

Treasurer

Financial Management

  • Manage organization budget and expenses
  • Process reimbursements and financial requests
  • Prepare financial reports for E-Board
  • Coordinate fundraising efforts
  • Apply for grants and university funding
  • Ensure financial transparency and accountability

Social Media Chair

Communications

  • Manage all social media platforms
  • Create engaging content calendar
  • Design graphics and promotional materials
  • Monitor and respond to online engagement
  • Track analytics and growth metrics
  • Coordinate with Creative Director on branding

Creative Director

Creative Leadership

  • Oversee visual branding and design consistency
  • Lead creative wellness workshops (art, music, writing)
  • Design merchandise and promotional materials
  • Coordinate creative therapy sessions
  • Develop innovative programming ideas

Events Coordinator

Event Planning

  • Plan and execute all organizational events
  • Coordinate logistics, venues, and supplies
  • Manage event budgets and timelines
  • Recruit and coordinate event volunteers
  • Evaluate event success and gather feedback

General E-Board Expectations

Commitment

Attend weekly E-Board meetings, fulfill role responsibilities, and maintain good academic standing while serving

Communication

Respond to messages within 48 hours, communicate challenges openly, and maintain professional relationships

Collaboration

Work as a team, support fellow E-Board members, and contribute to a positive organizational culture

Innovation

Bring fresh ideas, seek continuous improvement, and adapt to meet community needs

Questions about these roles? We'd love to hear from you!
